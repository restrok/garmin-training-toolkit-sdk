# Training Plan Generation Guidelines

## Core Principles (Based on Exercise Science Research)

### 1. Training Intensity Distribution (80/20 Rule)
- **80%** of training at Zone 2 (easy, conversational pace)
- **20%** at high intensity (Zone 4/5 intervals)
- Avoid spending time in Zone 3 (threshold) - neither recovery nor stress

### 2. Heart Rate Zones (Based on Max HR = 193)
| Zone | HR | Description |
|------|-----|-------------|
| Z1 | 97-116 bpm | Recovery |
| Z2 | 116-135 bpm | **Aerobic base (main zone)** |
| Z3 | 135-154 bpm | Tempo (avoid - gray zone) |
| Z4 | 154-174 bpm | Threshold |
| Z5 | 174-193 bpm | VO2max intervals |

### 3. Weekly Structure
```
Week 1: Medium load   (+5% volume)
Week 2: High load     (+10% volume)  
Week 3: High load     (same volume)
Week 4: **DELOAD**    (-40% volume)
Repeat cycle
```

### 4. Workout Types
| Type | Duration | Frequency | Zone |
|------|----------|-----------|------|
| Easy Run | 30-60 min | 3-5/week | Z2 |
| Long Run | 60-120 min | 1/week | Z2 |
| Intervals | 30-45 min | 1-2/week | Z5 |
| Tempo | 20-40 min | 1/week | Z4 |
| Rest | - | 1-2/day | - |

### 5. Recovery Rules
- No hard days back-to-back
- Easy days must be truly easy (Z1/Z2 only)
- Deload week every 4th week
- Listen to HRV/RHR for daily adjustments

### 6. Progression
- Increase volume by ≤10% per week
- Add intensity gradually after 4-6 weeks base
- Long run increases by 10-15% every 2 weeks

### 7. Periodization Phases
1. **Base (4-8 weeks):** Z2 only, build aerobic foundation
2. **Build (4-6 weeks):** Add intervals, tempo
3. **Peak (2-4 weeks):** Race-specific pace work
4. **Taper (1-2 weeks):** Reduce volume, maintain fitness

### 8. Training Stats to Consider
- Current weekly volume (km)
- Training frequency (runs/week)
- VO2 Max level
- Recent injury history
- Recovery markers (RHR, HRV if available)
- Sleep quality

### 9. Red Flags (Reduce training)
- RHR elevated >5 bpm
- Persistent fatigue
- Performance decline
- Injury or pain

## Quick Reference

- **Zone 2 pace:** Can hold full conversation
- **Tempo pace:** Can speak 4-5 words
- **Interval pace:** Cannot speak
- **Recovery HR:** Measure first thing in morning
- **Sleep:** 7-9 hours minimum

*These guidelines are based on current exercise physiology research including studies from Frontiers in Physiology, Sports Medicine journal, and ACSM guidelines.*
